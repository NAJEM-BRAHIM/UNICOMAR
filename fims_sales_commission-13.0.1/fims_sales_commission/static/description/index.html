Key Features.
Sales Commission on Sales Order.

User can configure commission while creating sales order, commission will be given based on sales person.

Sales Commission on Invoice.

While validating invoice commission will be calculated based on sales person.

Sales Commission Configuration.

Some configuration after installing this module.

First you have to give access rights to the user to use functionality of this module will be visible to that user.

Types of Commissions.

1) Standard Commission.

2) Partner Based Commission.

3) Product/Category Based Commission.

Standard Commission.

Standard commission is a basic commission you just add the percentage of commission for selected sales person.

Partner based Commission.

In Partner based Commission you can define two different percentage of sales commission based on partner.

1) Affiliated Partner.

2) Non-Affiliated Partner.

Product/Category Based Commission.

NOTE: When product and category commission are both in one product then first priority is category based commission.

1) Calculation based on Product:

If you select calculation based on Product sales commission will be given on price_unit of product.

2) Calculation based on Category:

If you select calculation based on Product the sales commission will be given on price_unit of product category.

Fix Price.

Sales commission applied on fix price.

Commission Percentage.

It will give the percentage of sales commission.

Commission based on sales order.

When you want to give commission while the sale order is being confirmed you can select this condition. find the sales commission line under the tab of sales commission in sales order form.

Commission based on invoice.

When you want to give commission based on invoice is being validated. find sales commission lines under the tab of sales commission in invoice form.

Commission based on refund/return.

When invoice was refund/return then find sales commission lines under the tab of sales commission in invoice form.
